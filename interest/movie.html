MR. & MRS. Smith

Alex's all time fav !!! "Mr. & Mrs.Smith" is a high-octane action-comedy film that follows the story of a married couple who lead secret double lives.

Directed by Doug Liman, "Mr. & Mrs. Smith" is an action-comedy film released in 2005. The story revolves around a married couple, portrayed by Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie, who are unaware that they are both secret assassins working for competing organizations. As they are tasked to eliminate each other, their relationship is put to the ultimate test. The film features a blend of action, humor, and romance, making it a thrilling and entertaining ride for audiences.

The chemistry between Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie, both on and off-screen, adds depth and intensity to their characters, creating a captivating dynamic throughout the movie. With its fast-paced action sequences, witty dialogue, and unexpected plot twists, "Mr. & Mrs. Smith" keeps viewers on the edge of their seats from start to finish.
